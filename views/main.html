INFORMED CONSENT FOR DENTAL TREATMENT

Please read carefully and initial each section

Blue border = Required  —  All initialed sections and the signature below must be completed before submitting.

TREATMENT TO BE DONE

I understand and consent to have any treatment done by the dentist after the procedure, the risks & benefits & cost have been fully explained.

DRUGS & MEDICATIONS

I understand that antibiotics, analgesics & other medications can cause allergic reactions.

CHANGES IN TREATMENT PLAN

I understand that during treatment it may be necessary to change or add procedures because of conditions found while working on the teeth that were not discovered during examination.

RADIOGRAPH (X-RAY)

I understand that an x-ray shot or a radiograph may be necessary as part of diagnostic aid.

INFORMED CONSENT - Page 2

Specific Procedures & Risks

Blue border = Required  —  All initialed sections and the signature below must be completed before submitting.

REMOVAL OF TEETH (EXTRACTION)

I understand the alternatives to tooth removal and completely understand the risks & benefits prior to authorizing the dentist to remove teeth.

CROWNS (CAPS) & BRIDGES

I understand that preparing a tooth may irritate the nerve tissue in the center of the tooth, leaving the tooth extra sensitive.

ENDODONTICS (ROOT CANAL)

I understand there is no guarantee that a root canal treatment will save a tooth and that complications can occur from the treatment.

PERIODONTAL DISEASE

I understand that periodontal disease is a serious condition causing gum & bone inflammation and/or loss that can lead eventually to the loss of my teeth.

INFORMED CONSENT - Page 3

Final Procedures & Authorization

Blue border = Required  —  All initialed sections and the signature below must be completed before submitting.

FILLINGS

I understand that care must be exercised in chewing on fillings, especially during the first 24 hours to avoid breakage.

DENTURES

I understand that wearing dentures can be difficult. Sore spots, altered speech & difficulty in eating are common problems.
